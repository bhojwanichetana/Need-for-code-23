WHAT WE DO

About Us

At ‘KHOJ’ , we beleive that each and every pet deserves a roof over its head and proper medical assistance. We are a non profit organisation working for the wellbeing of pet animals . In the last year we saved around 860 pets from dying and out of them 552 were adopted by people. Our organisation has made collaborations with various NGOs working in the similar field and are expanding each day. We are 24/7 online for the safety of animals

Providing Help

We are helping to stop cruelity towards animals in the following ways-:
We are rescuing the animals if the animals are injured or ill and give them proper medicine and proper treatment.
We give proper shelter for the animals so that they can stay comfortably.
Proper food and diet is provided so that animals will be healthy and do not fall ill in the further life.
We recommend you to not buy a pet from a pet store or a breeder ,instead adopt one from an animal shelter or adoption center. If you are really kind hearted, just pick up a pup on the side of the street and take him home.
Take a dog from the street into your own home. If that is not possible, try taking care of his food, medicines and vaccination.

Organising Animal Welfare Camps

An animal health camp was organized in the month of december where we collaborated with various non profit organisations and brought maximum number of ill animals at a health centre . They were treated and put up for adoption on our website , the animals which were not adopted were distributed to the NGOs and were taken care of .
